### 2. Mini Style Guide

**Brand Voice:** Premium, confident, clear, and professional. We empower users with powerful tools, and our language should reflect that.

**Capitalization:**
*   **Headings & Titles:** Use Title Case (e.g., `Workspace Settings`).
*   **Buttons & CTAs:** Use Title Case (e.g., `Get Started`, `Contact Sales`).
*   **Product/Module Names:** Always capitalize (e.g., `Gigaviz`, `Meta Hub`, `Core OS`).

**Punctuation:**
*   Avoid exclamation points in headings and body copy to maintain a professional tone. They are acceptable in success/confirmation messages (e.g., `Message Sent!`).
*   Use periods at the end of full sentences in body copy and descriptions.

**Calls to Action (CTAs):**
*   Start with a strong, clear verb.
*   Be concise and action-oriented.
*   Examples: `Get Started`, `View Pricing`, `Request a Demo`, `Contact Sales`, `Upgrade Plan`.

**Do / Don't List:**
*   **Do:** Use active voice (e.g., "Manage your workspace" instead of "Your workspace can be managed").
*   **Do:** Focus on user benefits (e.g., "Analyze trends and performance" instead of "View charts").
*   **Do:** Keep sentences and headings short and scannable.
*   **Don't:** Use jargon or overly technical terms unless necessary. Prefer simple words (`use` instead of `utilize`).
*   **Don't:** Use hype-y or exaggerated marketing language (e.g., "supercharge," "revolutionary").
*   **Don't:** Mix Indonesian and English in the same sentence or component.
