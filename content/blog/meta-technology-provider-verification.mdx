# How Gigaviz Became a Verified Technology Provider for the WhatsApp Business Platform

Modern messaging isn’t “just chat” anymore. For businesses, it’s the front door: support, sales, collections, renewals, confirmations—everything happens inside a message thread. But scaling messaging the wrong way is risky: policy violations, unreliable delivery, broken onboarding, and systems that collapse when you grow.

Gigaviz started with a simple idea: **turn chaotic business chats into a controlled, auditable system**—built to scale, not to “just work today.”

And in early 2026, our journey reached a key milestone: **our Technology Provider verification steps for the WhatsApp Business Platform were completed** (as shown in our public proof page).

## Why We Built on the Official WhatsApp Business Platform (Cloud API)

There are many ways people try to “automate WhatsApp.” Most shortcuts don’t survive real business scale.

We chose the official WhatsApp Business Platform (Cloud API) because we wanted:

- **Policy-aligned messaging** (the foundation for long-term reliability)
- **A stable infrastructure layer** for onboarding and messaging at scale
- **A clean audit trail** that businesses can trust
- **Room to grow** (workspaces, roles, billing, automation, integrations)

Gigaviz is not built as “a bot.” It’s built as **infrastructure**.

## The Early Problem: Messaging Chaos vs. Real Operations

Before Gigaviz, the pattern was always the same:

- Chats scattered across devices and admins  
- No shared inbox ownership  
- No consistent automation rules  
- No audit logs and no accountability  
- A workflow that depends on one person “who knows everything”  

This is fine… until it isn’t.

We wanted an opposite approach:

**One system. Clear roles. Traceable actions. A single source of truth.**

That became the direction of the Gigaviz Ecosystem.
