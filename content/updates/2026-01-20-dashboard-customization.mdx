We've shipped a new dashboard customization feature that puts you in control.

## What's new

- **Customize Quick Access**: Click the "Customize" button to select which modules appear in your dashboard
- **Pin up to 6 modules**: Choose from Platform, Meta Hub, Helper, Studio, and more
- **Per-user preferences**: Each team member can customize their own dashboard layout
- **Real-time sync**: Changes apply immediately across all your sessions

## How to use it

1. Open your dashboard
2. Find the "Quick access" section
3. Click "Customize"
4. Select 1-6 modules you use most
5. Click "Save Changes"

Your pinned modules are saved automatically and will appear every time you log in.
