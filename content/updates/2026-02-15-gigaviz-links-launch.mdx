We're excited to launch **Gigaviz Links** — a smart link-in-bio tool built for businesses on WhatsApp and social media.

## What's included

- **Bio Pages**: Create beautiful, branded landing pages at `gigaviz.com/l/your-name`
- **5 Link Types**: Regular URLs, WhatsApp chat links, product cards, social profiles, and section headings
- **Click Analytics**: Track every click with device breakdown, daily trends, and top-performing links
- **Theme Customization**: 8 built-in presets or fully custom colors, button styles, and corner radius
- **Drag & Drop Editor**: Reorder links instantly with smooth drag-and-drop (powered by dnd-kit)
- **WhatsApp Integration**: One-tap WhatsApp links with pre-filled messages — perfect for customer inquiries
- **Product Showcases**: Display products with images, descriptions, and prices directly on your bio page
- **QR Code Generator**: Download branded QR codes for print materials, packaging, or storefronts
- **SEO Ready**: Custom meta titles, descriptions, and dynamic OG images for every page
- **Rate-Limited Tracking**: Click endpoint protected against inflation attacks (60 req/min per IP)

## How it works

1. Go to **Links** in your workspace sidebar
2. Create a new page — choose a unique slug
3. Add links, products, or WhatsApp buttons
4. Customize your theme under **Page Settings → Appearance**
5. Hit **Publish** — your page is live instantly

## Who it's for

- **UMKM & small businesses** sharing their WhatsApp catalog on Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook
- **Freelancers & creators** who need a simple landing page for all their links
- **Sales teams** sending personalized bio pages with tracked WhatsApp links to prospects

## Technical details

- 3 database tables for pages, items, and click tracking — all workspace-scoped with RLS
- 6 API routes with full Zod validation and error handling
- Dynamic OG image generation using Next.js ImageResponse (1200×630, theme-aware)
- Privacy-preserving unique visitor tracking for accurate analytics

Gigaviz Links is available now for all workspaces with the `links` entitlement.
